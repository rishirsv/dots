Key findings – COVID-19 recoverability (2/2)
The film content and entertainment industry has seen a cumulative EBITDA increase of 194.0% from FY20 to FY23, and similarly a 72.8% increase in EBITDA margins
from an average of (54.6)% in FY20 to an average of 18.2% in FY23. However, the industry’s profitability still isn’t up to pre-COVID levels with EBITDA margins down by
3.95% compared to an average EBITDA margin of 22.2% in FY19.
