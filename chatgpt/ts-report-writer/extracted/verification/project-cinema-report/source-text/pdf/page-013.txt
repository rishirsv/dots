Key findings – COVID-19 recoverability (1/2)
The Company recorded a TTM Mar-24 reported EBITDA of $289.6 million which is 28.9% less than its FY19 reported EBITDA of $407.6 million. However, TTM Mar-24
EBITDA was affected by production delays related to the impact of the writers’ and actors’ strikes from May 2nd, 2023 to September 27th, 2023. Therefore, YTD Q3-23
EBITDA results of $287.4 million compared to Q3 YTD-19 EBITDA results of $297.1 million are more indicative of the Company’s COVID-19 recoverability.
