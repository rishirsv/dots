Better concession marketing
In comparison to its direct competitors, the Company has fewer options in its concessions,
and an apparent lack of marketing effort. The Company could feature a page dedicated to
communicating its menu offerings, and encourage patrons to purchase concessions prior to
their visit to theatres. Additionally, concessions can be more significantly imbedded in the
Company’s broader marketing strategy, especially considering its high margins.
