Key findings – Location based entertainment
Management is strategically focusing on diversification through their location based entertainment (LBE) segment to broaden their revenue streams and reduce reliance
on traditional cinema operations. LBE revenues makes up 9.2% of total Company revenue, a 1.2% increase since FY21. LBEs saw an increase in adjusted store level
EBITDA by 19.9% from FY22 to FY23, and an adjusted store level EBITDA margin increase of 0.1% However, in the TTM Mar-24 period, the company experienced a
decrease in adjusted store level EBITDA by 6.4%.
