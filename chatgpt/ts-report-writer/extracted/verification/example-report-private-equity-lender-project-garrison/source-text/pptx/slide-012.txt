Financial information
The activities of the business for sale are recorded in the books and records of a number of legal entities directly or indirectly owned by GE Capital or its subsidiaries. Individual detailed trial balances are not maintained for each component included in the transaction perimeter. Additionally, financial records for each component were not separately identifiable (i.e., distinct profit center identification) within the books and records of the related legal entity prior to 2013. Due to these system limitations, the financial statements and underlying financial information of Target were prepared using a combination of system-generated reports, quarterly management reporting to the Parent and management judgment.
During fieldwork, Management have stated that additional investment may be required to improve financial reporting.
To
understand the process undertaken by management to prepare the financial information provided over the historical period used in the course of due diligence, we met with Target management to understand the process to generate the financial information and to agree it to underlying source documentation. Our focus was on the detailed financial statements provided by Management (any summary financial statements or other financial information provided as part of this sale process were reconciled back to the detailed financial statements).
The financial information provided in the dataroom would represent the financial performance and position of Target after giving consideration to the adjustments made by Management to the underlying accounting records.
We recommend that during post-announcement pre-close, carve-out financial statements of the acquired business for the year-ending December 31, 2014 and quarter-ending March 31, 2015 are prepared and subjected to appropriate closing due diligence procedures.
