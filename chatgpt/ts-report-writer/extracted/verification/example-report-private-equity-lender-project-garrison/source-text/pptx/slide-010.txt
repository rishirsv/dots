FAS 91:
Amortization of FAS 91 loan origination costs increased from $11 million in 2013 to $35 million in 2014.
In Q1’13, Target began deferring and amortizing into its income statement FAS 91 costs. Previously, this was done at Parent and no amounts were pushed down to Target. The amounts in the respective income statements reflect that practice beginning in 2013
