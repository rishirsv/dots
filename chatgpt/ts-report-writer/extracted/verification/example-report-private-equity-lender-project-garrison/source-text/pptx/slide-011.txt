Fee income is a key driver in generating earnings for Target and is projected to continue to assume a greater proportion of total revenue over the forecast period. Upfront fees, driven by volume, and management fees, driven by assets under management within the SSLP and MMGP programs, are the primary components of fee income. Certain upfront fees are deferred and amortized into earnings over the stated life of the loan, creating a timing mismatch between cash receipts and revenue recognition. This timing mismatch should be considered in your valuation model.
