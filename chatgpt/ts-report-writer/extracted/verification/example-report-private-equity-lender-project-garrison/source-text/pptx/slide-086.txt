Impact of Deconsolidation
Generally
, when members join in filing a consolidated return, the taxable income and losses of group members are combined to arrive at the group’s net taxable income or loss.  However, when deconsolidation occurs, the tax attributes of the group, including group losses, should be distributed back to individual group members.
As
Target is currently a member of the GE US tax consolidated group, a determination of tax attributes separately allocable to the Target post-acquisition will need to be made.
