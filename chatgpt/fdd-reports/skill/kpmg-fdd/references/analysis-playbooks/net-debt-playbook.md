# Net debt and debt-like playbook

## Goal
Identify net debt and debt-like items that impact purchase price and closing mechanics.

This playbook aligns with the diligence checklist workstream “Debt and debt-like items”.

## Inputs to request
- Debt schedules / bank statements
- Cash detail (restricted vs unrestricted)
- Accrued liabilities detail
- Lease obligations summary (if relevant)
- Any seller-prepared net debt schedule

## Core analyses
1) **Debt schedule**
- Summarize instruments and balances; note maturity and rate if known.

2) **Debt-like items review**
- Identify items that may behave like debt at close.
- Document conclusions and basis.

3) **Cut-off considerations**
- Note timing risks and close-date movements.

## Tie-outs and checks
- Confirm cash definition matches deal mechanics (if provided).
- Ensure sign conventions are consistent (debt increases net debt).

## Output artifacts
- Net debt schedule
- Debt-like checklist and conclusion table
- Open items list for confirmations
