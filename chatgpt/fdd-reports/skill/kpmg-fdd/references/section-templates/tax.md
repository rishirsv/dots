# Tax template

## Purpose
Summarize tax structure, exposures, and key diligence questions.

## Recommended structure

### Tax profile
- Entity structure overview (if available)
- Jurisdictions and filing footprint (if available)

### Known exposures and areas to diligence
- Uncertain tax positions, audits, aggressive positions (if described)
- Payroll, sales/VAT, transfer pricing (as relevant)

### Implications
- Potential purchase agreement protections (escrows, indemnities) — keep qualitative unless directed otherwise

### Open items
- Tax returns, notices, audit correspondence, NOL schedules, etc.

## Suggested exhibits
- Tax exposures table (issue, status, potential magnitude, mitigant, source/basis)

## Common pitfalls
- Writing definitive conclusions without tax evidence.
