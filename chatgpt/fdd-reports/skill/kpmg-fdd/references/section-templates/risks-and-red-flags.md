# Risks and red flags template

## Purpose
Present the key risks clearly, ranked, with mitigants and next steps.

## Recommended structure

### Risk register (ranked)
Use a table:
- Risk
- Severity (deal-breaker / significant / manageable)
- What we observed
- Why it matters (value/terms)
- Mitigant / next step
- Status (open / in progress / resolved)

### Red flags escalation notes
If a red flag exists:
- State what was found
- Evidence basis
- What is needed to resolve
- Potential deal term implications (qualitative if needed)

## Common pitfalls
- Burying risks in footnotes or vague language.
- Minimizing risks (credibility hit).
