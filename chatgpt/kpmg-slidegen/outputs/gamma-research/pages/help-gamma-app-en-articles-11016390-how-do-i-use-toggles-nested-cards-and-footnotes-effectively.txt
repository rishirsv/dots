The dashboard is the main workspace hub where you can access your gammas, manage folders, explore templates, browse themes, get inspired, and find support settings - all in one place.  How to Use the Gamma DashboardStep 1: Access Your GammasFrom the left-hand panel, select Home, and then Gammas to view the gammas you have access to.Use filters to sort by All, Recently viewed, Created by you, Favorites, and switch between Grid View and List View depending on your preference.💎 Pro Tip: Pin your most-used gammas to Favorites for quick access when working on multiple projects. Step 2: Use the Search FunctionQuickly find the gamma you're looking for, using our search functionality.Step 3: Check Out Shared with YouView gammas made by others that have been shared with you. Use filters to sort by All, Recently viewed, and Favorites. You can also switch between Grid View and List View depending on your preference.Step 4: Explore Your SitesYou’ll find any Gamma sites that you’ve created in this section, but you’ll also see them in your general All Gammas view as well.To learn more, check out: How do I create & publish a site in Gamma?Step 5: API GeneratedAnything you've generated with the API can be found in this section. To learn more, check out:Does Gamma have an API?Step 6: Stay Organized with FoldersClick the + next to Folders to create a new folder.To move a gamma into a folder, drag and drop it into the desired folder.🔷 Note: A gamma can belong to multiple folders.To remove it from a folder, open the folder, click on the gamma, then select Remove from folder.💎 Pro Tip: Use folders to separate work by project, team, or content type.Step 7: Access the TrashNavigate to Trash to restore or permanently delete any discarded gammas.Step 8: Browse TemplatesTemplates: Browse different layout styles. Click Add a gamma to add your first template.To learn more, please see:How do I use Workspace Templates?Step 9: Access Your LibraryThemes: Explore pre-designed themes with custom fonts, colors, and images. You can also create your own themes.Custom Fonts: Gamma includes Google fonts by default, but you can upload your own for custom branding. My AI images: Here you can find images you've already generated with AI, and can generate images directly from this page also. Learn more about the AI Image Dashboard here:About the AI Image DashboardLearn more about themes and custom themes here: How do I change my Gamma theme?Step 10: Manage Workspace SettingsUnder Settings & Members, you can manage team roles and permissions.To learn more, check out: Accounts, Workspaces & User SettingsStep 11: Contact Customer SupportClick the ? in the bottom-right corner of the dashboard.Select Contact Support to chat or send an email.
