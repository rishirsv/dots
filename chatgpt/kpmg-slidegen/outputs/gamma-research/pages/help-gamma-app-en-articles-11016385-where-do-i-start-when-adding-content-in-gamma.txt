Option 2: Use Agent to Generate and Edit ContentClick the sparkle icon on a selected card, or click Agent at the top of the presentationChoose whether to refine existing content or generate new content.If generating new content, enter a prompt or select AI-generated suggestions.Apply the AI-generated text to your gamma. Option 3: Use the Slash Command to Add ContentClick inside a card and type /.A menu of available content options will appear (e.g., GIFs, images, code blocks, team photos).Select an option to insert it directly into the card. Option 4: Drag Content from the Insert MenuOpen the Insert Menu on the right-hand side of the screen.Drag and drop images, videos, embeds, or other media into your gamma.Adjust the content placement as needed.
