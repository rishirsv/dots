Instead of starting from scratch, you can import documents or presentations directly into Gamma and turn them into editable Gamma cards.🌀 Insight: Importing works with a wide range of sources - including files, webpages, and Google Drive - so you can quickly jumpstart your work using content you’ve already written. You can bring material in manually or with help from Gamma's AI Agent, making the setup fast and seamless. How to Import Slides or Docs into GammaStep 1: Choose an Import MethodYou can choose between two import options: Option 1: Plain ImportUse this to pull in text content exactly as-is.From your Gamma home screen, click Import.Select the file type or platform you’re importing from (e.g., PowerPoint, Google Docs, Webpage URL, Notion).Gamma will import the text into cards - one card per slide (for slides) or one per heading (for docs). Option 2: Import with AIUse AI to restyle or transform your content during import.Click New with AI from the home screen.Select Import.Paste your content or upload a file.Choose your theme and layout preferences.Gamma AI will generate a newly styled presentation for you. Step 2: Make AdjustmentsOnce imported, you can:Edit the contentAdd visualsUse AI chat to rewrite or reformat cardsApply a new theme or design layoutDebugging Import IssuesIf something didn’t import correctly:Try exporting your file as .docx or .pptx before importing.You can also copy and paste directly into Gamma if the importer fails.Use /split between sections to divide content into separate cards. FAQs & Common IssuesWhy didn’t my styles or layouts import?Gamma currently only supports importing text. Styling and layout will need to be added manually or by using AI.My Google Drive import isn’t working - what can I do?Export the file as a Word (.docx) or PowerPoint (.pptx) file and try importing that instead.Can I import content from Notion?Yes! You can import public Notion docs, or simply copy and paste directly into Gamma.How can I break up long imported text?Use /split in your content to create a new card where you want a section
