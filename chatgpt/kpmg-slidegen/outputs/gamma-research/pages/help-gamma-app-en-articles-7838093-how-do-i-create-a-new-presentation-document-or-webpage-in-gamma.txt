Whether you need a structured first draft, want to refine existing text, or transform uploaded files, Gamma provides multiple options to streamline content creation! What is Gamma’s Creation Feature?🌀 Insight: Gamma provides three different modes to help users create visually appealing and structured content.Generate – AI drafts an initial version based on your topic.Paste – AI formats and refines pre-written content.Import – AI converts existing files, decks, or web pages into a structured format.How to Create a New Presentation, Document, or WebpageStep 1: Access the Gamma DashboardNavigate to your Gamma dashboard.Click Create New AI.Choose from the available creation modes: Generate, Paste, or Import. Step 2: Choose the Right Mode for Your NeedsGenerate (Best for First Drafts & Brainstorming)Enter a topic, and Gamma will generate a structured draft.Use Advanced Mode to refine the draft further.Paste (Best for Formatting Existing Text)Copy and paste your text (from notes, ChatGPT, or other sources).Gamma will format it into a clean, fresh layout.Import (Best for Converting Existing Files or URLs)Upload a PowerPoint, document, or enter a URL.Gamma will import your content and style it automatically. Step 3: Edit and Refine Your ContentAfter creation, click the sparkle icon in the top-left of any card to edit with AI.AI will suggest layout refinements and content improvements.To edit multiple cards at once, use the sparkle icon in the top-right of your screen - available for Pro users. How to Generate Without Images or Use PlaceholdersWhen generating a new gamma, you have the flexibility to tailor the visual style from the start:No Images: Choose Don’t add images to generate a clean, text-only gamma.Image Placeholder: Choose Image placeholders to leave visual space you can fill in later with your own content.🔷 Note: This gives you more creative control and helps streamline your workflow, whether you’re focusing on copywriting or planning to insert final visuals later! FAQs & Common Issues Why didn’t the AI generate what I expected?The AI models that Gamma uses are still improving and learning from user input.Try refining the input topic or adjusting the structure using "Advanced Mode".➡️ To learn more, check out About Writing AI Image PromptsCan I edit my Gamma after creating it?Yes! Click the sparkle icon to refine content with AI.Currently, AI editing only applies to individual cards and content - unless you are a Pro user.You can also manually adjust text, images, and layouts.➡️ To learn more about editing your Gamma content, check out Editing Designing & Formatting in GammaDoes Gamma support theme changes?Yes, you can change your theme at any time by selecting
