You can manually add new cards, generate them using AI, and customize their layout with text, images, media, and other elements.🌀 Insight: Unlike static slides, Gamma’s cards dynamically adjust to fit your content while maintaining an organized layout.Key Features of CardsCards can be manually added or generated using AI.They automatically adjust in height to fit your content, but can be set to a fixed size.Card sizes can be controlled under Page Settings.Users can apply templates for quick formatting. 💎 Pro Tip: If a card grows too tall, consider moving some content to a new card for better readability.How to Add a New CardManually Adding a CardNavigate to an existing Gamma document or create a new one.Hover between two existing cards until a + appears.Click the + to add a new blank card. How to Add a Card Using AgentClick the sparkle icon to open Agent's AI suggestions.If your Gamma has existing content, Agent will suggest additional cards based on related topics.If none of the suggestions fit your needs, manually enter a topic for Agent to generate content.Choose a preferred language style and template for the new card.Click Generate to add the new card to your Gamma. How to Hide a CardYou can choose to hide individual cards in your Gamma presentations and sites. Just right-click on the card in the filmstrip and click Hide. Hidden cards stay in your presentation or site but won’t appear while presenting, or in the published website. Bring them back anytime by right-clicking and selecting Unhide.💎 Pro Tip: Use hidden cards to store backup content or alternate versions without deleting anything. How to Edit Cards - A Quick Hack!Gamma now makes it easier to edit cards with a simple right-click.Go to your Gamma and right-click on the edge or padding of any card - this brings up a quick-access menu just for that card.🌀 Insight: From here, you can instantly make changes like copying cards, re-styling, adding notes, and quickly changing layouts. It’s a fast way to stay in the flow while editing without clicking through extra menus!   FAQs & Common IssuesCan I add multiple cards at once?Not at the moment. Each card must be added individually, either manually or using AI.Are there templates for cards?Yes! Click the down arrow next to the sparkle icon to explore available card layouts, or drag and drop a fresh layout from the insert menu.Is there a limit to how many cards I can add?No, you can add unlimited cards. However, if you are using Agent on the Free plan, your AI credits may limit how many AI-generated cards you can create. You can always add cards manually without any restrictions.How do I control card sizes?By default, card are fluid, meaning they automatically resize based on the content inside them.You can adjust the minimum aspect ratio under the three-dot menu (…) in the top right corner, and then clicking Page Setup.If you select a fixed ratio (e.g., 16:9), dotted guidelines will appear to help you manage your content.Related ArticlesHow do I create a new presentation, document, or webpage in Gamma?Where do I start when adding content in Gamma?How do I use toggles, nested cards, and footnotes effectively?Can I translate a Gamma document?How do I copy a card from one Gamma to another?Did this answer your question?😞😐😃
