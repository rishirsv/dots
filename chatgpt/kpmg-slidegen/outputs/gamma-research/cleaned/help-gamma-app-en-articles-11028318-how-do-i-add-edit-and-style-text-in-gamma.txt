Updated over 4 months agoTable of contentsIntroductionHow to Edit Text in GammaStep 1: Select a Card to EditStep 2: Format Your TextAdditional Text FeaturesAdding FootnotesAdding LinksUsing Lists & BlockquotesFAQs & Common IssuesIntroductionGamma makes it easy to add, edit, and style text within your gamma. 🌀 Insight: With a simple text formatting menu, you can adjust font size, color, alignment, and add elements like links, lists, and footnotes. How to Edit Text in GammaStep 1: Select a Card to EditOpen your gamma.Click on any card to begin editing.Start typing directly into the card.Step 2: Format Your TextHighlight the text you want to format.A text formatting menu will automatically appear above.Choose from the available formatting options:Font size – Adjust text size using the menu or by typing / and selecting a size.Font color – Pick from predefined colors or create a custom theme for additional options.Text formatting – Apply bold, italic, underline, or strikethrough.Alignment – Align text left, center, or right. Additional Text FeaturesAdding FootnotesHighlight the text where you want to add a footnote.Click Add Footnote from the text formatting menu.A footnote will appear at the bottom of the card where you can enter your reference.To learn more, check out: How do I use toggles, nested cards, and footnotes effectively?Adding LinksHighlight the text you want to hyperlink.Click Insert Link in the text formatting menu.Enter the URL and confirm.Using Lists & BlockquotesSelect text and choose bulleted or numbered lists from the formatting menu.For blockquotes, type > before your text or select Blockquote from the menu.💎 Pro Tip: Use blockquotes to make testimonials, quotes, or key points stand out. It’s a great way to add visual contrast without breaking the flow of your content. FAQs & Common IssuesWhy can’t I edit text on my mobile device?Currently, Gamma only supports text editing on desktop. Mobile editing is not available yet.Can I apply custom colors to my text?Yes! You can select from available colors or create a custom theme for more options.How do I change font styles or upload my own font?Custom fonts are available for Pro users.
