Style settings include image position, background color, and mask overlays.🌀 Insight: By adjusting style settings, you can create a gamma that’s visually engaging and easy to read (for smaller screens like your phone if needed). How to Use Accent ImagesClick the top-left corner of the card you want to edit and select Card styling.Above Accent image, choose a layout: No imageTopRightLeftBackgroundOnce selected, click Edit to change the image, or select Remove accent image to delete it.💎 Pro Tip: To quickly change an accent image, just double-click directly on the image!Change up your slide layouts by seamlessly positioning product photos, icons, or illustrations alongside your text.To learn more about accent images, check out:   What’s the easiest way to crop or resize images in Gamma?How do I add and edit images in Gamma? How to Use a Background Image OverlayIf you want the background of your card to be a photo (instead of a solid color), you can use this method:In the same Card styling pop-up, choose Background from the Accent image layout options.This unlocks styling options - select between Frosted, Faded, or Clear overlay styles.Adjust the overlay intensity anywhere from 0%–100% to change how much the background image shows through.Finally, choose your overlay color to match your theme or improve contrast.💎 Pro Tip: Use darker overlays + light text for maximum readability on mobile.This layout feature is ideal for hero slides, quotes, or image-heavy moments where you want the text to sit cleanly over a background photo. How to Change a Card’s Background ColorIf you’d prefer a solid color instead of a photo background:In the Card styling pop-up, scroll to the Card color section.Choose a preset from your theme or pick any color to apply a solid background.💎 Pro Tip: Stick with high contrast for better visibility on phones or when screen recording.This layout feature is great for emphasizing key ideas, breaking up content sections, or using brand colors in your layout. How to Make a Full-Bleed CardWant your card to take over the entire screen, edge-to-edge? That’s a full-bleed card.In the same Card styling pop-up, toggle on the Full-bleed card option. How to Align Your ContentSet how your text and visuals sit within a card:In the Card styling pop-up, go to Content alignment and choose:Top align contentCenter align contentBottom align contentAdjusting alignment helps balance your layout visually, especially when mixing cards with images and text blocks. To learn more about styling card headers & footers (available to Pro users only), check out: How can I add headers and footers to my gamma? Extra Tips for Mobile-Friendly LayoutsUse large fonts in your design theme for easy reading.Limit to one idea per card for clarity on smaller screens.Always preview in full screen to test mobile readability.FAQs & Common IssuesCan I apply styling to all cards at once?Not yet - each card is styled individually. But you can duplicate styled cards and swap the content for consistency.If you are a Pro user, you can also apply some types of styling to all cards at once by clicking on the top right Edit all cards with AI, and ask AI to eg. “add right accent images to all of the cards”What’s the difference between background color and background image?Background color is a solid color fill, while a background image lets you set a photo or visual behind your content. You can use overlays to help text stand out when using background images.Why can’t I see my text clearly on a background image?Try adjusting the overlay style or intensity, or choosing a different image with more negative space. You can also switch to a solid background color for higher contrast.Does changing card styling affect the rest of the gamma?Nope - card styling is per-block. You can freely mix and match layouts across your gamma depending on the story you want to tell.How do I make my cards look more consistent?Use the same alignment, background style, and image positioning across key sections - or create a styled card you like and duplicate it as a starting point.Related ArticlesWhat are cards in Gamma and how to do they work?How do I add, edit, and style text in Gamma?How do I add and edit images in Gamma?How do I control card sizes and adjust page setups?How do I generate images with AI in Gamma?Did this answer your question?😞😐😃
