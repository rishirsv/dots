# Accounting policies and audit history template

## Purpose
Highlight reporting quality indicators and policy sensitivities that could affect earnings and balance sheet items.

## Recommended structure

### Audit history and reporting quality
- Audited vs unaudited periods
- Known restatements or qualification notes (if mentioned)

### Key accounting policies to confirm
- Revenue recognition approach (if relevant)
- Capitalization vs expensing policies (capex, software dev costs, etc.)
- Reserve policies (bad debt, inventory obsolescence) if applicable

### Implications
- Where policy differences could impact reported metrics or comparability

### Open items
- Audit reports, management letters, policy memos, revenue recognition documentation

## Suggested exhibits
- Accounting policy checklist with status (received / in review / open)
