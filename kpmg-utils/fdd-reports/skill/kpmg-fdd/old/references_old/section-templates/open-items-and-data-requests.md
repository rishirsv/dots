# Open items and data requests template

## Purpose
Make missing information explicit, prioritized, and actionable.

## Recommended structure

### Open items table
| Priority | Item | Why it matters | Needed for | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Priority guidance:
- **P0**: gates a conclusion or could materially change an outcome
- **P1**: improves precision, reduces range/uncertainty
- **P2**: nice-to-have, completeness

### Seller responsiveness watch-outs
If the seller is slow to respond, note it as a diligence risk signal (without over-interpreting).

### Next steps
- What is needed this week
- What can wait

## Common pitfalls
- Open items list exists but has no “why it matters”.
- No prioritization, making it unusable in deal team discussions.
