# P&L overview template

## Purpose
Summarize historical performance and drivers, and set up the QoE normalization.

## Recommended structure

### Historical performance summary
- Revenue, gross profit, EBITDA (and margins) across the period
- Volatility commentary (what changed, when, and why)

### Key drivers
- Price vs volume vs mix (only if evidenced)
- Major cost drivers and margin movement
- Any seasonality or concentration effects (if supported)

### Data quality notes
- Whether financials are audited vs management reporting
- Any known restatements or reclasses (if mentioned)

## Suggested exhibits
- Summary P&L table (multi-year + LTM if available)
- Trend chart (optional) for revenue and EBITDA margin
- Margin bridge table (if drivers are clear)

## “Do not do” list
- Do not assert a driver (“mix drove growth”) without evidence.
- Do not mix periods (FY + LTM) without explicitly labeling and explaining.
