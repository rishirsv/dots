# Capex template

## Purpose
Assess maintenance vs growth capex and implications for normalized earnings and cash flow.

## Recommended structure

### Historical capex pattern
- Capex trend (period-aligned)
- Major projects or spikes (if described)

### Maintenance vs growth
- Management view vs analytic view (label assumptions)
- Evidence (asset register, project lists) if available

### Implications
- Sustainability of current spend
- If capex is likely understated, flag impact on free cash flow

### Open items
- Capex detail by category, asset register, depreciation policy notes

## Suggested exhibits
- Capex trend table/chart
- Maintenance vs growth classification table
