# Net debt and debt-like items template

## Purpose
Identify net debt and debt-like items relevant to purchase price and closing adjustments.

## Recommended structure

### Debt schedule
- Instrument, balance, key terms (rate/maturity if available)
- Commentary on unusual items or covenants (if known)

### Cash considerations
- Cash definition (restricted vs unrestricted)
- Cut-off timing considerations (close-date movement)

### Debt-like items review
Provide a table of items reviewed and conclusion:
- Deferred compensation, accrued bonuses, lease obligations, customer deposits, legal reserves, etc. (tailor to available evidence)

### Key risks and open items
- Missing confirmations, unclear classification, seller responsiveness delays

## Suggested exhibits
- Debt and cash schedule
- Debt-like items checklist and conclusion table

## Common pitfalls
- Treating “net debt” as only bank debt minus cash without reviewing debt-like items.
- Not labeling cut-off timing risks.
