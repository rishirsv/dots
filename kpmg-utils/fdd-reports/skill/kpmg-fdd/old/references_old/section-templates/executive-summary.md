# Executive summary template

## Purpose
Give the decision-maker the key conclusions quickly: what we found, what it means, what could change.

## Recommended structure

### 1) Deal and scope at a glance
- Target company, deal type, periods covered
- Workstreams in-scope vs out-of-scope

### 2) Key conclusions by workstream (bullets)
Minimum set (if in-scope):
- QoE / earnings normalization
- Working capital and peg implications
- Net debt / debt-like items

### 3) Top risks and mitigants
- 3–7 risks, ranked by severity
- For each: what it is, why it matters, mitigant / next step

### 4) Open items that could move conclusions
- P0 items gating conclusions
- P1 items to tighten ranges / confirm assumptions

## Suggested exhibits
- One-page reported-to-adjusted EBITDA bridge (if available)
- Summary table of open items with owner and ETA

## Common pitfalls
- Purely descriptive summary (“we reviewed X”) with no findings.
- No open items list (creates false confidence).
