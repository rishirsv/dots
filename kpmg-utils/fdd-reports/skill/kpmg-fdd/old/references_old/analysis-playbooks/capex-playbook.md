# Capex playbook

## Goal
Assess maintenance vs growth capex needs and implications for normalized earnings and cash generation.

This playbook aligns with the diligence checklist workstream “Capital expenditure (maintenance vs. growth)”.

## Inputs to request
- Historical capex by period
- Fixed asset register / project lists (if available)
- Depreciation schedules (if available)
- Management capex plans and budgets

## Core analyses
1) **Trend and context**
- Identify spikes and explain based on evidence (projects, expansions).

2) **Maintenance vs growth**
- Separate where possible; otherwise present ranges and assumptions.

3) **Implications**
- Note whether maintenance capex appears understated/overstated relative to depreciation, with caveats.

## Output artifacts
- Capex trend exhibit
- Maintenance vs growth classification exhibit (or open item)
