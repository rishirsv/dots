# QoE playbook

## Goal
Produce a defensible view of normalized earnings by identifying and supporting QoE adjustments (revenue and EBITDA adjustments).

This playbook aligns with the diligence checklist workstream “Quality of earnings (QoE) — revenue and EBITDA adjustments”.

## Inputs to request (use what is available)
- Historical financials (IS by month/quarter if possible)
- Trial balance or general ledger extracts (if available)
- Management adjustment schedule (if available)
- Non-recurring cost details (one-time fees, settlements, restructuring)
- Revenue detail (by customer/segment if relevant)

## Core analyses
1) **Bridge reported to adjusted**
- Build a reconciliation: reported EBITDA → adjustments → adjusted EBITDA.
- Categorize adjustments by type and label recurrence.

2) **Revenue quality checks (narrative)**
- Identify unusual spikes, cut-off issues, or concentration if evidence exists.
- If evidence is not available, list as an open item rather than asserting.

3) **Margin bridge drivers (when evidence exists)**
- Explain what drove margin changes (price, mix, volume, costs).
- Use the management meeting prep question set as a guide for what to probe.

## Tie-outs and checks
- Confirm the bridge sums correctly.
- Ensure EBITDA definition is consistent across exhibits.
- Ensure periods align (FY vs LTM).

## Output artifacts
- Adjustment bridge table (primary exhibit)
- Adjustment support schedule for top items (appendix)
- Sensitivities and open items list
