# Canonical playbook: Business overview

## Objective
Produce a business overview that is structurally consistent, decision-useful, and aligned to earnings drivers.

## Analysis sequence
1. Define operating perimeter and business model components.
2. Map offerings to revenue mechanics and major cost drivers.
3. Map customers to channels and commercial terms.
4. Map operating dependencies that can affect margin quality.
5. Map period changes to trend context.

## Output checks
- Does each block answer its intended question?
- Are statements specific and period-aware?
- Is missing data handled with placeholders instead of omissions?
- Is there any unnecessary deep P&L commentary in this section?
