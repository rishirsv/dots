# Canonical playbook: QoE and earnings adjustments

## Objective
Produce a reported-to-adjusted EBITDA bridge with supportable adjustment logic and clear sensitivity framing.

## Analysis sequence
1. Lock periods and EBITDA definition.
2. Build reported baseline.
3. Catalogue and classify adjustments.
4. Quantify adjustments consistently.
5. Draft rationale and residual risk by adjustment type.
6. Summarize sensitivities.

## Output checks
- Does the bridge tie arithmetically?
- Is each material adjustment explained clearly?
- Are unresolved values represented with placeholders?
- Are definitions and period labels consistent end-to-end?
