## Verification Checks

The verification script (`verify_data_room.py`) validates:

### Universal Checks
- Trial balance exists
- Trial balance balances (debits = credits)
- Balance sheet equation (A = L + E)
- Revenue consistency (IS = TB)
- AR aging ties to BS
- AP aging ties to BS
- Headcount ties to seed
- Cash flow ending cash ties to BS cash
- Net income ties to retained earnings change
- Payroll totals tie to payroll-linked financial accounts
- Rendered narratives have no unresolved template tokens

### Industry-Specific Checks
- **SaaS:** MRR ties to revenue, ARR = MRR × 12
- **Construction:** WIP revenue ties to P&L
- **Manufacturing:** Invoice totals tie to revenue
- **Services:** Billed revenue ties to P&L
- **Retail:** Transaction totals tie to revenue
- **Dental:** Collections tie to revenue, insurance AR ties to BS AR, utilization/productivity ranges
