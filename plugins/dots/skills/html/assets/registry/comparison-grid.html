Managed queue

Hosted broker, autoscaled consumers, dashboards out of the box. Fastest to stand up, least control over retry semantics.

best for: teams without spare ops bandwidth

Self-hosted worker pool

Recommended. Our own retry and backoff logic on top of a plain job table — no new infra, and we already run the failure paths we need.

best for: this migration, given our current retry requirements

Serverless functions

Pay-per-invocation, zero idle cost. Cold starts and a 15-minute execution ceiling rule out our longest ingest jobs.

best for: bursty, short-lived jobs
