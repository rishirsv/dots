# Product Spec Template

```md
# <Feature Or Product Area> Product Spec

## TL;DR

<The short version of what we are building and why.>

## Scope

<The product boundary in one or two paragraphs.>

## What We Are Building

- <User-visible capability, workflow, or behavior.>

## How It Works

<The core workflow, rules, data flow, or user interaction model in plain language.>

## User-Visible Surface Map

- <Screen, flow, command, document, or touchpoint affected.>

## Non-Goals

- <What this spec explicitly does not include.>

## Key Decisions

- <Decision or tradeoff settled during scoping.>

## Open Questions

- <Unresolved question, or `None`.>

## Acceptance Criteria

- <Observable outcome that proves the product behavior works.>

## Verification Notes

- <Tests, manual checks, review steps, or artifact checks that should validate the work.>
```
