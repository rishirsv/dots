## 1.0 Corporate, Organization, Ownership and Financing

1.1 Provide legal entity chart, including all subsidiaries, DBAs, branches and inactive entities.
1.2 Provide charter documents, bylaws, LLC agreements and all amendments.
1.3 Provide good standing and foreign qualification certificates.
1.4 Provide board/shareholder minutes, written consents and committee materials for the last 3 years.
1.5 Provide cap table, shareholder register, option/warrant/convertible schedules and equity-plan documents.
1.6 Provide all debt agreements, promissory notes, LOCs, guaranties, security agreements and covenant packages.
1.7 Provide bank account list, authorized signers and treasury/cash policies.
1.8 Provide related-party agreement register and copies of all related-party contracts.
1.9 Provide historical acquisition, divestiture, JV and restructuring documents.

## 2.0 Financial Information

2.1 Provide audited/reviewed/compiled financial statements for the last 3 fiscal years and latest interim period.
2.2 Provide monthly management P&L, balance sheet and cash flow for the last 36 months plus latest YTD.
2.3 Provide monthly trial balance by entity and account for the same period.
2.4 Provide general ledger transaction detail and journal entry register for the same period.
2.5 Provide chart of accounts and account-mapping files.
2.6 Provide revenue detail by month and by primary operating unit: customer, contract, project, SKU, store, provider, route or equivalent.
2.7 Provide gross margin support by major revenue stream, product/service line, location or project.
2.8 Provide AR aging, reserve support and top overdue account explanation.
2.9 Provide AP aging, accrued liabilities support and top vendors.
2.10 Provide bank statements and reconciliations for the last 12 months.
2.11 Provide debt schedule, covenant calculations and compliance certificates.
2.12 Provide fixed asset register, capex history, depreciation schedule and lease/rent schedule.
2.13 Provide budget, forecast, board plan and variance analyses.
2.14 Provide adjusted EBITDA bridge and support for all non-recurring, owner and run-rate adjustments.
2.15 Provide NWC support schedules and proposed peg methodology if available.

## 3.0 Tax

3.1 Provide federal, state and local income tax returns for the last 3-5 years.
3.2 Provide sales/use/VAT/GST and other indirect tax returns for the last 24 months.
3.3 Provide payroll tax and property tax filings for the last 24 months.
3.4 Provide tax provision/tax basis workpapers and depreciation/amortization schedules.
3.5 Provide NOL, credit, Section 174/R&D and other tax-attribute schedules.
3.6 Provide nexus/apportionment analysis and any transfer-pricing files.
3.7 Provide all tax notices, audit correspondence and settlement documentation.
3.8 Provide list of open tax years and uncertain tax positions.

## 4.0 Commercial, Sales, Customers and Marketing

4.1 Provide customer master and top-50 customer summary.
4.2 Provide customer concentration by month and year, including top-10 and top-20 views.
4.3 Provide standard pricing/rate cards and discount/rebate policies.
4.4 Provide executed contracts and amendments for top customers and any strategic/channel partners.
4.5 Provide order, booking, billing and invoice data for the last 24-36 months.
4.6 Provide pipeline/CRM export, bookings/backlog and win/loss data.
4.7 Provide returns, credits, rebates, warranty claims and chargeback schedules.
4.8 Provide marketing spend by channel and major campaign performance.
4.9 Provide customer retention/churn/cohort metrics where relevant.
4.10 Provide any customer NPS, satisfaction or quality-of-service reporting.

## 5.0 Operations, Delivery, Supply Chain and Service Quality

5.1 Provide operating model overview and key KPI dashboard.
5.2 Provide site/facility/service-location list and basic operating characteristics for each.
5.3 Provide supplier master, top-vendor spend and copies of material supplier/procurement agreements.
5.4 Provide inventory, BOM, production, utilization, project or service-delivery data, as applicable.
5.5 Provide backlog/WIP/retainage files if project-based.
5.6 Provide maintenance logs, capex backlog and major equipment list.
5.7 Provide quality, complaint, warranty, rework, return or service-level data.
5.8 Provide operating seasonality narrative and any major disruption history.

## 6.0 Human Resources and Management

6.1 Provide org chart and management biographies.
6.2 Provide employee census including title, location, manager, hire date, compensation and status.
6.3 Provide payroll registers for the last 24-36 months.
6.4 Provide bonus, commission, incentive and equity award schedules.
6.5 Provide employment agreements for executives and key employees.
6.6 Provide severance, CIC, retention and non-compete arrangements.
6.7 Provide benefits-plan documents, renewal history and retirement-plan materials.
6.8 Provide turnover metrics, open positions and hiring plan.
6.9 Provide employee claims, labor disputes, union/CBA materials and immigration/licensure rosters if applicable.

## 7.0 Technology, Product, IP, Data and Cybersecurity

7.1 Provide application landscape and IT systems inventory.
7.2 Provide product roadmap, release history and major development initiatives if relevant.
7.3 Provide cybersecurity policies, DR/BCP, incident register and recent assessments.
7.4 Provide critical-system user access listings and privileged-access summaries.
7.5 Provide privacy policies, DPA/BAA register and material data-sharing arrangements.
7.6 Provide IP register, domain inventory and copies of key registrations/assignments.
7.7 Provide third-party software/license inventory and any material outsourcing agreements.
7.8 Provide compliance attestations such as SOC 2, PCI, HIPAA, ISO, FedRAMP or equivalents, if applicable.

## 8.0 Legal, Material Contracts and Litigation

8.1 Provide standard customer and vendor templates.
8.2 Provide top customer, supplier, distributor, reseller and strategic partnership agreements.
8.3 Provide lease agreements, amendments and abstracts for all material locations.
8.4 Provide financing/guaranty/security documents not already uploaded in Section 1.
8.5 Provide litigation, arbitration, threatened claims and settlement files.
8.6 Provide related-party agreements and affiliate arrangements.
8.7 Provide license/permit register and copies of material permits.
8.8 Provide copies of any government contracts, franchise agreements, payer contracts or other sector-specific material agreements.

## 9.0 Regulatory, Compliance, Quality and EHS

9.1 Provide master list of permits, licenses, certifications and accreditations.
9.2 Provide inspection, examination, audit and accreditation reports for the last 3 years.
9.3 Provide compliance manuals, SOPs, QA manuals and training logs.
9.4 Provide incident, complaint, CAPA, recall and remediation logs.
9.5 Provide EHS logs, OSHA/EPA correspondence and site environmental files, if applicable.
9.6 Provide AML/KYC, sanctions, privacy, reimbursement or sector-specific monitoring reports where applicable.

## 10.0 Real Estate, Facilities and Environmental

10.1 Provide site list with address, use, square footage, owned/leased status and start/end dates.
10.2 Provide lease abstracts and full executed leases for all leased locations.
10.3 Provide deeds, title policies, surveys and zoning for owned property.
10.4 Provide rent roll, occupancy and facilities capex schedules.
10.5 Provide maintenance, repair and utility summaries for key facilities.
10.6 Provide environmental reports, remediation files and other site-condition studies.

## 11.0 Insurance and Risk Management

11.1 Provide current policy schedule and binders.
11.2 Provide loss runs/claims runs for the last 3-5 years.
11.3 Provide broker summary, renewal terms and premium history.
11.4 Provide details on self-insured retentions, captives and material uninsured exposures.
11.5 Provide sector-specific coverage such as malpractice, product liability, builders risk, cargo or cyber, if applicable.

## 12.0 Process, Transaction and Diligence Workpapers

12.1 Provide CIM, management presentation and any banker or lender presentation.
12.2 Provide data room index, request tracker and Q&A log.
12.3 Provide vendor diligence reports: QoE, tax, legal, commercial, IT/cyber, EHS, reimbursement or other workstreams if prepared.
12.4 Provide draft SPA/APA/merger agreement and disclosure schedules index, if available.
12.5 Provide management response memos for major diligence findings.
12.6 Provide any board materials relating to sale process, recapitalization or strategic review.
