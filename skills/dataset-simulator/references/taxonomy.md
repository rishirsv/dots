## 1.0 Corporate, Organization, Ownership and Financing

**Subsections:** 1.1 Legal Entity Structure | 1.2 Organizational Documents | 1.3 Governance and Approvals | 1.4 Ownership/Equity/Securities | 1.5 Debt/Banking/Liens | 1.6 Related Party and Corporate Actions

### Universal Artifacts
- Legal entity chart
- Jurisdiction list
- Subsidiaries/branches/DBAs list
- Articles of incorporation
- Bylaws
- LLC agreements
- Good standing certificates
- Board minutes and consents
- Shareholder ledger/cap table
- Equity incentive plan documentation
- Option ledger
- Shareholder/investor rights and voting agreements
- Debt schedule
- Credit agreements, promissory notes, lines of credit, security agreements, guaranties
- UCC searches, lien searches, payoff letters
- Bank account list with authorized signers
- Treasury policy
- Related-party register and agreements
- Historical acquisition, divestiture, and JV documentation

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- **Ownership structure memo** – varies by founder-led vs. PE-backed vs. roll-ups vs. physician groups vs. regulated financial entities
- **Financing package** – ABL, covenant-heavy debt, securitizations, equipment financing, bonding

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- MSO/PC or DSO agreements (healthcare)
- Franchise agreements and FDD (franchise)
- Regulatory capital requirements and holding company approvals (financial services/utilities)

## 2.0 Financial Information

**Subsections:** 2.1 Audited/Reviewed Statements | 2.2 Monthly/Quarterly Management Accounts | 2.3 TB/GL/JE | 2.4 Revenue and Gross Margin Support | 2.5 BS Support and Working Capital | 2.6 Cash/Debt/Banking | 2.7 Capex/Fixed Assets/Leases | 2.8 Budget/Forecast/Board Plan | 2.9 Adjusted EBITDA and QoE Support

### Universal Artifacts
- Audited, reviewed, or compiled financial statements
- Monthly management P&L, BS, and cash flow statements
- Monthly trial balance by entity and account
- General ledger detail
- Journal entry listing
- Chart of accounts and account mapping
- Revenue bridge by month and year
- AR aging and bad debt analysis
- AP aging and accrued liabilities
- Cash reconciliations and bank statements
- Debt schedule and covenant calculations
- Fixed asset register and depreciation schedules
- Capex history and budget
- Net working capital schedules
- Budget, forecast, and variance analysis
- Adjusted EBITDA bridge
- Calendarization, TTM, and stub period bridges

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- **Revenue detail:**
  - SaaS: MRR, ARR, cohorts, deferred revenue
  - Construction: job-cost, WIP, retainage
  - Manufacturing: SKU, order, inventory
  - Services: engagement, utilization, realization
  - Retail: store, SKU, POS data
  - Dental: provider, procedure, payer, collections
- **Gross margin by:** project, customer, site, store, SKU, provider, or service
- **Working capital detail:** deferred revenue, retainage, inventory, insurance AR
- **KPI packs:** churn/NRR (SaaS), backlog, same-store sales, utilization, payer mix

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- CECL reserve, delinquency analysis (lending)
- Statutory capital schedules (insurance/regulated)
- Reserve reports, hedge accounting, PPA analysis (energy)
- Reimbursement waterfalls (provider)

## 3.0 Tax

**Subsections:** 3.1 Income Taxes | 3.2 Indirect Taxes | 3.3 Payroll/Property/Local Taxes | 3.4 Tax Attributes and Planning | 3.5 Tax Audits/Notices/Controversy

### Universal Artifacts
- Federal and state income tax returns
- Tax provision and basis workpapers
- Depreciation and amortization schedules
- Payroll tax filings
- Property tax filings
- State apportionment and nexus analysis
- Tax correspondence, notices, and audits
- Open audit status summary
- NOL and credit schedules

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- Sales/use/VAT/GST filings (footprint varies by jurisdiction and business model)
- Transfer pricing documentation (if international operations)
- R&D credits, Section 41, Section 174, Section 263A documentation (if applicable)
- Marketplace, telecom, or excise tax filings (if applicable)

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Premium tax (insurance)
- Fuel/excise/customs duties (transport/import)
- Severance and royalty taxes (natural resources)
- Reimbursement tax programs (healthcare)

## 4.0 Commercial, Sales, Customers and Marketing

**Subsections:** 4.1 Customer Base and Concentration | 4.2 Contracts and Pricing | 4.3 Pipeline/Bookings/Backlog | 4.4 Marketing and Channels | 4.5 Retention/Claims/Returns/Discounts

### Universal Artifacts
- Customer master file
- Top-customer list
- Customer concentration analysis
- Contract register
- Pricing and rate cards
- Discount, rebate, and credit schedules
- Pipeline and CRM data
- Order and invoice history
- Channel partner list
- Marketing spend by channel
- Win/loss summaries

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- **SaaS:** cohort retention, logo/dollar churn, expansion revenue, bookings vs. billings
- **Construction:** backlog, WIP schedules, change orders, claims, retainage analysis
- **Manufacturing:** order history, price-volume mix analysis, freight costs, channel performance
- **Services:** client backlog, utilization rates, realization, project burn
- **Retail:** same-store sales, basket analysis, traffic metrics, markdowns, returns
- **Dental:** payer mix, referral sources, new patient acquisition, treatment acceptance, collections

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Government contract backlog and revenue
- Franchise royalties and comparable sales
- Managed care and payer rate schedules
- Loan and portfolio origination funnels

## 5.0 Operations, Delivery, Supply Chain and Service Quality

**Subsections:** 5.1 Operating Model Overview | 5.2 Delivery/Production/Service Execution | 5.3 Procurement and Suppliers | 5.4 Inventory/Capacity/Utilization | 5.5 Quality/Service Levels/Maintenance

### Universal Artifacts
- Operating KPI dashboard
- Site and facility list
- Supplier master file
- Spend concentration analysis
- Procurement policies
- Equipment list and utilization schedules
- Maintenance backlog
- Complaint, warranty, and rework summaries
- Operating calendar and seasonality analysis

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- **Manufacturing:** BOMs, routings, scrap rates, OEE, purchase price variance
- **Construction:** project master, job-cost ledgers, subcontractor roster, equipment tracking
- **Services:** timesheets, engagement register, utilization tracking
- **Retail:** store master, inventory by location, shrink analysis, labor scheduling
- **SaaS:** support tickets, uptime metrics, implementation schedules, release cadence
- **Dental:** provider roster, schedule utilization, chair utilization, collections tracking
- **Logistics:** route density, on-time performance, fleet metrics

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- HACCP and lot traceability (food/pharma)
- Batch records and CAPA logs (regulated manufacturing)
- DOT, FAA, or marine logs (transportation)
- Outage and generation logs (energy)

## 6.0 Human Resources and Management

**Subsections:** 6.1 Organization and Census | 6.2 Payroll and Variable Compensation | 6.3 Benefits and Retirement | 6.4 Agreements and Policies | 6.5 Employee Relations and Claims

### Universal Artifacts
- Organizational chart
- Leadership biographies
- Employee census
- Payroll registers
- Bonus plan documentation and payout history
- Commission plan schedules
- Equity awards and vesting schedules
- Employment agreements for key employees
- Offer letters, change-in-control, severance, and retention agreements
- Employee handbook and HR policies
- Benefits plan documentation
- Turnover analysis, open requisitions, and headcount plan
- Employee claims and disputes summary

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- Billable-rate schedules (services)
- Productivity-based compensation (provider)
- Shift staffing and overtime policies (labor-intensive)
- Union contracts and CBA (where applicable)
- Credentialing and licensure records (licensed businesses)

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Physician, dentist, and provider employment contracts
- CDL, pilot, and maritime license rosters
- Security clearance documentation
- Residency and fellowship materials

**Subsections:** 7.1 Systems Landscape | 7.2 Product and Development | 7.3 Cybersecurity | 7.4 Data Privacy and Governance | 7.5 IP and Licensing | 7.6 Third-Party Software and Vendors

### Universal Artifacts
- Systems and application landscape
- ERP, CRM, and HCM inventory
- IT organization and vendor list
- Cybersecurity policies
- Incident log
- Disaster recovery and business continuity plans
- User access listings
- Software license inventory
- IP register (patents, trademarks, domains, copyrights)
- IP assignment and confidentiality agreements
- Privacy policy and data-sharing register

### Industry-Variable Artifacts

- **SaaS:** SLA documentation, uptime metrics, support structure
- **Industrial:** OT/ICS controls and documentation
- **Manufacturing/Logistics:** EDI and integration mappings
- **Retail:** e-commerce platform stack

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- SOC 1/2 audit reports
- ISO 27001 certification
- PCI compliance documentation
- FedRAMP authorization
- HITRUST and HIPAA files
- Source code escrow agreements
- FDA software validation documentation
- AI and model governance frameworks
- Telecom and network diagrams

**Subsections:** 8.1 Standard Forms | 8.2 Material Customer/Vendor Contracts | 8.3 Strategic and Special Agreements | 8.4 Litigation and Claims | 8.5 Licenses/Permits/Related Party

### Universal Artifacts
- Standard customer terms and conditions
- Standard vendor terms and conditions
- Material customer contracts and amendments
- Material supplier contracts and amendments
- Partnership, reseller, and distributor agreements
- Lease agreements and amendments
- Financing, guaranty, and security agreements
- Litigation summary and status
- Pleadings, settlements, and claims documentation
- Related-party agreements
- Permit and license register

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- MSA, SOW, and order forms (SaaS/services)
- Supply and distribution agreements (product)

- Payer, provider, referral, and affiliation agreements (healthcare)
- Franchise agreements (franchise)

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Government contracts
- PPAs, offtake agreements, and interconnection agreements (energy)
- Clinical trial, CRO, and quality agreements (life sciences)
- Servicing and subservicing agreements (financial services)

## 10.0 Real Estate, Facilities and Environmental

**Subsections:** 10.1 Site List | 10.2 Leased Locations | 10.3 Owned Property | 10.4 Facilities Capex and Maintenance | 10.5 Environmental

### Universal Artifacts
- Site list with square footage and use
- Lease abstracts and full lease documentation
- Rent roll
- Deeds, title documents, and surveys (if owned)
- Zoning and occupancy permits
- Facilities capex and maintenance records
- Utility summaries and cost analysis

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- **Manufacturing:** plant layouts and equipment positioning
- **Healthcare:** clinic room counts and configuration
- **Distribution:** warehouse capacity and throughput
- **Retail:** store footprint analytics
- **Services:** office utilization metrics
- **Asset-heavy:** equipment scheduling by site

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Phase I and Phase II environmental assessments
- Remediation files and status
- Tank inspections and certifications
- Mine and mineral rights documentation
- Tower and site license agreements
- Cold storage validation reports

## 11.0 Insurance and Risk Management

**Subsections:** 11.1 Current Coverage | 11.2 Historical Claims | 11.3 Broker and Renewal Materials

### Universal Artifacts
- Policy schedule and binders
- Loss runs and claims runs
- Broker summary
- Renewal terms and proposals
- Certificates of insurance
- Self-insured retention (SIR) summary

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- **Common across most:** D&O, EPLI, cyber, property, general liability
- **Variable by model:** malpractice, product liability, builders risk, cargo, fleet, warranty, business interruption

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Malpractice tail coverage (healthcare)
- Aviation and marine hull insurance
- Environmental impairment liability
- Clinical trial insurance
- Surety and bonding (construction)

## 12.0 Process, Transaction and Diligence Workpapers

**Subsections:** 12.1 Seller Materials | 12.2 Vendor Diligence | 12.3 Buyer Diligence Workstreams | 12.4 SPA/APA and Disclosure Schedules | 12.5 Q&A and Tracker Files

### Universal Artifacts
- Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM)
- Management presentation
- Data room index
- Request tracker and IRL tracker
- Q&A log
- Diligence contact list
- Draft SPA and APA
- Disclosure schedule index
- Vendor quality-of-earnings, tax, legal, commercial, and IT reports

### Industry-Variable Artifacts
- Sector-specific diligence workstreams expand/contract:
  - Reimbursement (healthcare)
  - Cyber (software/SaaS)
  - EHS (manufacturing/energy)
  - Government (defense/federal contractors)
  - Reserves (insurance/energy)
  - Clinical quality (life sciences)
  - Food safety (food/beverage)

### Industry-Specific Artifacts
- Actuarial reviews and opinion letters
- Engineering reserve reports
- Reimbursement compliance audits
- Grid studies and power flow analysis (energy)
- Environmental reserve assessments
- Food safety audits and certifications
